Simple Mindfulness Tips

Start Small: Even one minute of focused breathing can reset your mind.

Use Anchors: Focus on your breath, a specific sound, or a sensation in your body when your mind wanders.

Practice Non-Judgment: Don't criticize yourself if you get distracted. Gently redirect your attention back to your anchor.

Scheduled Time: Treat meditation like any other important appointment in your day.
