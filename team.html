Our Mission

We are a group of developers and wellness advocates dedicated to building simple, effective tools for self-care. WellTrack U was created to promote digital wellness literacy and support SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being. We believe tracking your mind and body is the first step toward a healthier, more balanced life.
